Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mia Snow ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Mia Snow Facebook Messenger chatbot ("the App") and handles information.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our Facebook Messenger chatbot, we receive and temporarily process the content of your messages in order to generate a response. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information beyond the content of your messages.

How We Use Your Information

Message content is used solely to generate automated responses through our chatbot. Conversation history is stored to allow the chatbot to maintain context within an ongoing conversation. We do not sell, share, or distribute your message content to any third parties.

Data Storage

Conversation history is stored securely in a database to support the chatbot's memory features. You may request deletion of your conversation history at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Third Party Services

Our chatbot uses the following third party services to operate:

  • Meta/Facebook — messaging platform
  • Anthropic — AI response generation
  • Render — application hosting
  • Supabase — database storage

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request access to, deletion of, or information about the personal data we have collected about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
